AUTOCAD Workshop:
AutoCAD is a computer-aided design software created by Autodesk that enables drafters, architects, engineers, and other experienced professionals to create two dimensional and three-dimensional models of solid surfaces and meshes. AutoCAD is widely used, and it helps in preventing product failures and warranty issues.

SOLIDWORKS Workshop:
Solidworks is the preeminent software for computer aided design and computer aided engineering, which is an industry standard for CAD designs and several benefits of the user. In today's competitive job market, CAD professionals need every advantage they can get and being efficient in SOLIDWORKS gives you a proven edge.

4D Printing Workshop:
4D printing refers to single-material or multi-material printing of a device or object that can be transformed from a 1D strand into pre-programed 3D shape, from a 2D surface into pre-programmed 3D shape and is capable of morphing between different dimensions. Benefits of 4D printing include: increased capabilities of the printed products; new applications from adaptive materials; added manufacturing efficiency; and, reduced manufacturing cost and carbon footprint.
